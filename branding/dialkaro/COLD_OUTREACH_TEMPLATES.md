# DialKaro — Cold Outreach Templates

Three channels: WhatsApp (highest reply rate in India), LinkedIn DM, Email.
Use **WhatsApp first**. Email last (most ignored).

---

## A. Cold WhatsApp — first touch

**Send from:** a dedicated WhatsApp Business number (not your personal).
Set the WhatsApp Business profile → "Sales @ DialKaro" with website link.

```

still copy-pasting lead numbers from Excel into their phone dialer?

I built DialKaro because watching that workflow drove me crazy.
60-second video showing how it works:

{Loom link}

If it solves a real problem for you, reply DEMO and I'll set up
your team in 5 minutes. Free trial, no card.

If not — no worries, ignore this. 🙏
```

### Reply playbook

| User replies | Your response |
|--------------|---------------|
| "DEMO" | "Great. Drop your email + how many reps. Tenant ready in 5 min." |
| "How much?" | "₹X/rep/month. Free for 14 days, no card. Want me to set up?" |
| "Not interested" | "All good 🙏 If your dialer ever breaks, we're here." (nothing else) |
| Long question | Reply with **voice note** — feels human, 4× higher conversion |
| No reply in 4 days | Send Follow-up F1 (below) |
| No reply after F1 | Stop. Don't spam. |

### Follow-up F1 — sent 4 days after no reply

```

{specific Loom link or screenshot — e.g., specialty routing}

If now's not the right time — reply STOP and I'll never message again.
```

## B. Cold LinkedIn DM — for B2B / larger orgs

LinkedIn replies feel slower (3-7 days) but sticks better.

### Template L1 — to Founder / CEO of mid-market sales-led SaaS

```
Hi {FirstName},

Saw your post about scaling the {company} sales team — quick
context, I built DialKaro (AI sales dialer for Indian SMBs) and
your team's exactly our profile.

Not pitching you — genuinely curious: how is your team handling
inbound lead-routing today? I keep seeing the same problem
(round-robin via WhatsApp groups → 30% leads never called) and
wonder if it's universal or just an SMB thing.

If you're open to a 15-min chat, happy to share what we've learned
across the customers we've worked with. No pitch.

Either way — keep building 🙏
```

```
If that's a problem worth solving for your team, here's a 60-sec
demo: {Loom link}

If your current setup is dialed in (pun intended), no worries 🙏
```

## G. The single most important rule

**Never sound like a sales person.**

Sales-y opener: ❌ "I hope this message finds you well. I wanted to introduce..."
Human opener: ✅ "Saw you run a DSA team in Pune. Quick question..."

Indian buyers smell a templated sales message in 2 seconds. Personalize:
- Their city
- Their team size (estimate from LinkedIn)
- Their industry (DSA vs real estate vs edtech — pick one template)
- A specific public detail (their post, their hiring page, their website)

The 30 seconds you spend personalizing each message → 5× the reply rate.
