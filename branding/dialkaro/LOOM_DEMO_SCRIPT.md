# DialKaro — 60-Second Loom Demo Script

This is the single highest-leverage marketing asset you'll record.
Used in: LinkedIn posts, cold WhatsApp, ads, sales emails, landing page.

**Total runtime:** 60-75 seconds.
**Tone:** calm, confident, zero hype, India-aware.

---

## Pre-recording checklist

- [ ] Use Chrome **incognito** — no extensions, no bookmarks bar visible
- [ ] Window at 1920×1080
- [ ] Sign in to a **demo tenant** with real-looking sample data (use "DialKaro Demo" tenant)
- [ ] Have a 50-row sample CSV ready (Indian names, +91 numbers — anonymized)
- [ ] Close all other tabs
- [ ] Disable system notifications (DND on macOS)
- [ ] Wear headphones — Loom captures keyboard clicks otherwise
- [ ] Test mic level → record a 5-sec test, listen back

---

## The script (read out loud)

### **[0:00 — 0:05]** Hook

> *Screen: dialkaro.celerapps.com landing page*
>
> "If you have a sales team that dials leads from Excel, your reps
> waste 60% of their day not selling. Let me show you DialKaro."

---

### **[0:05 — 0:15]** Upload + capacity

> *Action: drag-drop a 50-row CSV onto the dialer*
>
> "Upload any Excel or CSV — phone numbers in any format, +91, leading
> zero, hyphens, doesn't matter. DialKaro normalizes them all."

*Pause 1 sec while contacts load.*

> "50 leads, ready to dial."

---

### **[0:15 — 0:30]** The actual dialing

> *Action: click the phone-call button on lead #1*
>
> "One click — phone call. One click — WhatsApp Business with the
> message pre-filled."

*Show WhatsApp deep-link opening.*

> "Rep marks the outcome — interested, not interested, callback —
> and DialKaro auto-advances to the next contact. No copy-paste,
> no spreadsheet, no losing your place."

---

### **[0:30 — 0:42]** AI summary (the wow moment)

> *Action: click "Generate session summary"*
>
> "At the end of the session, Claude AI reads every call outcome and
> writes a one-paragraph summary — what worked, which leads to chase
> tomorrow, which to drop."

*Read the AI output on screen for 2-3 seconds.*

> "Managers used to spend 30 minutes doing this in a Google Doc. Now
> it's automatic."

---

### **[0:42 — 0:55]** Manager view + routing

> *Action: switch to admin dashboard*
>
> "On the manager side — every rep, every call, every outcome.
>
> And new leads from your website, Meta Ads, or IndiaMART webhook
> in here automatically — routed by specialty. Health-insurance
> leads only go to health-insurance reps. Automatic. No assignment
> meeting needed."

---

### **[0:55 — 1:05]** Close

> *Screen: pricing or signup page*
>
> "DialKaro. Built for Indian SMBs. Try it free at dialkaro.celerapps.com.
> Or message hello@celerapps.com — I'll set up your tenant in 5 minutes."

## Recording tips

1. **Don't write a tight script** — bullet points beat sentences. Speak naturally.
2. **Re-record 3-5 takes** — the 4th is always your best.
3. **Cut dead air** ruthlessly in Loom's editor (the trim tool).
4. **Don't apologize** ("uhh sorry the demo data is fake") — it leaks.
5. **End on a clear CTA** — never trail off.

---

## What NOT to include

- ❌ Long intro about your company history
- ❌ Pricing details (let them ask — friction-driven qualification)
- ❌ Tech stack name-drops (Supabase, Claude — only mention "AI", not the model)
- ❌ Too many features — 3 hero features is enough (upload, AI summary, routing)
- ❌ Any mention of you personally as founder
