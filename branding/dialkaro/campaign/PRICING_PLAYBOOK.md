## Free Trial Strategy

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Duration | **14 days** |
| Features | All features unlocked |
| Credit card required | **No** |
| Rep limit during trial | Up to 15 reps |
| Data retained after trial | Yes (for 30 days) |
| Automatic billing after trial | **No** — manual conversion only |

### Why 14 Days (Not 7 or 30)

- **7 days** → too short for team adoption, reps need 3-5 days to form habit
- **30 days** → too long, urgency dies, leads go stale
- **14 days** → enough for 2 full work weeks, creates natural deadline

## Value Anchoring — The Math That Sells

### For DSA / Loan Agents

```
Your team: 10 reps
Leads per day: 100
Leads never called: ~30% = 30 leads/day
Average commission per conversion: ₹5,000
Conversion rate on called leads: 3%

Lost revenue per day = 30 × 3% × ₹5,000 = ₹4,500
Lost revenue per month = ₹4,500 × 22 days = ₹99,000

DialKaro cost = ₹4,999/month
ROI = ₹99,000 / ₹4,999 = 20× return
```

### For Real Estate

```
Your team: 8 brokers
Leads per day: 50  
Leads never called: ~25% = 12 leads/day
Average brokerage per deal: ₹50,000
Conversion rate: 1%

Lost revenue per month = 12 × 22 × 1% × ₹50,000 = ₹1,32,000
DialKaro cost = ₹4,999/month
ROI = 26× return
```

### For EdTech

```
Your team: 15 counselors
Leads per day: 200
Leads never called: ~35% = 70 leads/day  
Average enrollment value: ₹15,000
Conversion rate: 2%

Lost revenue per month = 70 × 22 × 2% × ₹15,000 = ₹4,62,000
DialKaro cost = ₹4,999/month
ROI = 92× return
```

**Always present the math BEFORE the price.** The price becomes trivially small in comparison.

## Objection Responses — Pricing Specific

### "It's too expensive"

> *"I understand. Let me ask — how many leads does your team miss per day because of manual routing? Even if it's just 10 leads at ₹500 each commission potential, that's ₹5,000 per day. DialKaro costs ₹2,499 per MONTH. It pays for itself in half a day."*

### "We'll think about it"

> *"Totally fair. How about this — I'll set up a free trial right now. 14 days, all features, no card. Use it for a week with your team. If it doesn't save time, just stop logging in. No follow-up."*

### "Can you do ₹999/month?"

> *"For our first 10 customers, I can do ₹1,999/month locked in forever. That's the lowest we'll ever go, and it includes all features + direct WhatsApp support. Want me to set it up?"*

### "We use [free tool] / Excel / WhatsApp"

> *"Excel and WhatsApp are great for teams under 3 people. The moment you have 5+ reps and 50+ leads/day, manual routing breaks. That's not a tool problem — it's a volume problem. DialKaro solves the volume problem."*

### "What if we outgrow it?"

> *"You'll need to upgrade to the next plan tier. Pricing stays proportional — no enterprise tax. And if you ever hit 100+ reps, we'll work out a custom plan."*
