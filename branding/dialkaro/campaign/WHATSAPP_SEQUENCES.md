## Reply Handling Playbook

| They Reply | You Respond |
|------------|------------|
| **"DEMO"** | "Great! Drop your email + how many reps. Tenant ready in 5 min." |
| **"How much?"** | "₹2,499/mo for up to 5 reps. Free 14-day trial first. Want me to set up?" |
| **"Not interested"** | "All good 🙏 If your dialer ever breaks, we're here." (STOP — nothing else) |
| **"STOP"** | "Done ✅ You won't hear from me again. All the best!" (add to STOP list) |
| **Long question** | Reply with a **voice note** — feels human, 4× higher conversion |
| **"We use [CRM]"** | "DialKaro works alongside your CRM. Export from CRM → upload → dial. No replacement needed." |
| **"Send me details"** | Send: Loom link + 3-bullet summary + "Reply DEMO when ready" |
| **"Can I try first?"** | "Absolutely! Let me set up your trial right now. What's your team name?" |
| **Asks for discount** | "First 10 customers get early-adopter pricing locked forever. Let me check availability." |

## Timing Rules

| Day | Best Time to Send |
|-----|------------------|
| Monday | 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM (after they settle in) |
| Tuesday-Thursday | 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM or 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM |
| Friday | 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM only (people check out after lunch) |
| Saturday | 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (owners often work Saturdays) |
| Sunday | ❌ Never send on Sunday |

**Never send after 7 PM.** It feels intrusive.

## Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Every first message includes opt-out language ("reply STOP")
- [ ] Maintain a STOP list — never re-message
- [ ] Only use publicly listed business contact info
- [ ] Max 3 messages to a non-responding contact
- [ ] Don't add to WhatsApp broadcast lists without consent
- [ ] Keep all messages professional — no spam, no misleading claims
