# DialKaro — 30-Day Sales Playbook (First 10 Clients)

> **Goal**: 10 paying clients within 30 days of campaign start.
> **Status**: No active clients. Product is live at `dialkaro.celerapps.com`.
> **Owner**: CelerApps team.

---

## How to Use This Document

This is your **day-by-day operating manual**. Every file you need is linked inline. Don't skip steps — each day builds on the previous one.

Start on a **Monday**. The first week is pure setup. Outreach starts Day 8.

---

## ═══════════════════════════════
## WEEK 1 — FOUNDATION (Days 1-7)
## ═══════════════════════════════

### Day 1 — Record the Demo Video

**Why first**: Every outreach channel needs a visual asset. Nothing works without the Loom.

1. Follow [`DAY1_CHECKLIST.md`](../DAY1_CHECKLIST.md) step by step
2. Use [`LOOM_DEMO_SCRIPT.md`](../LOOM_DEMO_SCRIPT.md) for the recording
3. Upload [`demo-sample-leads.csv`](../demo-sample-leads.csv) during recording
4. Upload custom thumbnail [`loom-thumbnail-1280x720.png`](../loom-thumbnail-1280x720.png)
5. Get the public Loom URL → paste it everywhere below

**Deliverable**: One polished 60-sec Loom link, public.

---

### Day 2 — Set Up LinkedIn Showcase Page

**Why**: LinkedIn is where B2B decision-makers live. Your showcase page is your storefront.

1. Follow [`LINKEDIN_SHOWCASE_SETUP.md`](../LINKEDIN_SHOWCASE_SETUP.md)
2. Create the showcase at `linkedin.com/showcase/dialkaro`
3. Use the tagline, description, and specialties from the guide
4. Upload logo + banner (use CelerApps logo until DialKaro-specific one exists)
5. Paste your Loom link in the "About" section

**Deliverable**: Live LinkedIn Showcase page at `linkedin.com/showcase/dialkaro`

---

### Day 3 — Set Up WhatsApp Business + Email

**Why**: WhatsApp is the #1 sales channel in India. 8-12% reply rate vs 1-3% for email.

1. **WhatsApp Business App** → download from Play Store / App Store
2. Use a **dedicated number** (not your personal) — get a new SIM if needed
3. Set up profile:
   - Business name: `DialKaro by CelerApps`
   - Category: `Software`
   - Description: `AI sales dialer for Indian SMBs · 3× more calls per day`
   - Website: `https://dialkaro.celerapps.com`
   - Email: `hello@celerapps.com`
4. Set up auto-reply: "Thanks for reaching out! We'll get back to you within 2 hours."
5. Add the Loom link to your WhatsApp status

**Also set up**:
- Email signature with Loom link: `"60-sec demo: {Loom URL}"`
- Gmail label: `DialKaro Leads` for tracking inbound

**Deliverable**: WhatsApp Business profile live, email signature updated.

---

### Day 4 — Build Your Prospect List (200 contacts)

**Why**: Outreach starts Day 8. You need ammunition ready.

1. Open [`ICP_TARGET_LIST.md`](ICP_TARGET_LIST.md) — understand who to target
2. Open [`PROSPECTING_SCRIPTS.md`](PROSPECTING_SCRIPTS.md) — exact search queries
3. Scrape 200 contacts across 4 segments:

| Segment | Target | Source | Contacts |
|---------|--------|--------|----------|
| DSA / Loan Agents | 60 | IndiaMART + JustDial | Company, Name, Phone, City |
| Real Estate Brokers | 50 | JustDial + 99acres agents | Company, Name, Phone, City |
| EdTech / Coaching | 50 | Google + LinkedIn | Company, Name, Phone/Email |
| Insurance / NBFC | 40 | LinkedIn + Apollo.io | Company, Name, Email, LinkedIn |

4. Save as [`TRACKER_TEMPLATE.csv`](TRACKER_TEMPLATE.csv) format
5. **Load your own prospect list into DialKaro** — eat your own dog food! 🐕

**Deliverable**: 200-row prospect CSV, loaded into your own DialKaro instance.

---

### Day 5 — Publish LinkedIn Post #1

**Why**: Start building organic visibility before outreach begins.

1. Copy Post 1 from [`LINKEDIN_POSTS_WEEK1.md`](../LINKEDIN_POSTS_WEEK1.md)
2. Post **AS the DialKaro page** (not personal account)
3. Replace `[Loom link]` with your actual Loom URL
4. Add the Loom thumbnail as image attachment
5. Engagement: ask 5 friends to like + comment within 2 hours
6. Reply to every comment for 24 hours

**Deliverable**: LinkedIn Post #1 live with 5+ engagements.

---

### Day 6 — Prepare Outreach Templates

**Why**: Day 8 is outreach blitz. Templates must be personalized and ready.

1. Open [`COLD_OUTREACH_TEMPLATES.md`](../COLD_OUTREACH_TEMPLATES.md)
2. Customize all `{Loom link}` placeholders with your real URL
3. Open [`WHATSAPP_SEQUENCES.md`](WHATSAPP_SEQUENCES.md) — review full drip sequences
4. Pre-write 10 personalized WhatsApp messages (from your 200-list)
   - Each message must include: their city, their team size estimate, their industry
5. Pre-draft 5 LinkedIn DMs using template L1/L2
6. Pre-draft 5 cold emails using template E1

**Deliverable**: 20 messages pre-written, personalized, ready to send.

---

### Day 7 — Rest + Review

1. Review everything created this week:
   - [ ] Loom video live and public?
   - [ ] LinkedIn showcase page complete?
   - [ ] WhatsApp Business profile set up?
   - [ ] 200 prospects in CSV?
   - [ ] LinkedIn Post #1 published?
   - [ ] Templates personalized?
2. Fix anything incomplete
3. Read [`DEMO_FLOW.md`](DEMO_FLOW.md) — practice the live demo flow twice
4. Read [`PRICING_PLAYBOOK.md`](PRICING_PLAYBOOK.md) — know your pricing inside out

## WEEK 2 — COLD OUTREACH BLITZ (Days 8-14)
## ═════════════════════════════════════

### Daily Outreach Targets

| Channel | Daily Volume | Template Source |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **WhatsApp** | 50 messages | [`COLD_OUTREACH_TEMPLATES.md`](../COLD_OUTREACH_TEMPLATES.md) |
| **LinkedIn DM** | 10 messages | Templates L1, L2 |
| **Cold Email** | 20 emails | Template E1 |
| **Total** | 80 touches/day | |

### Day 8 — Outreach Day 1 (WhatsApp Focus)

**Morning block (9 AM - 12 PM)**:
1. Open DialKaro → load your prospect list
2. Start dialing/messaging the first 50 WhatsApp contacts
3. Use template W1 for DSA, W2 for Real Estate, W3 for EdTech
4. **Personalize each message** (30 seconds per message → 5× reply rate)
5. Log every response in your tracker

**Afternoon block (2 PM - 5 PM)**:
1. Send 10 LinkedIn DMs (template L1 or L2)
2. Send 20 cold emails (template E1)
3. Respond to ANY morning replies within 30 minutes

**Evening (7 PM)**:
1. Update your tracking spreadsheet
2. Note which messages got replies
3. Score each response: Hot / Warm / Cold / No Reply

---

### Day 9-10 — Continue Outreach + Publish Post #2

- Continue 80 touches/day
- Day 10: Publish LinkedIn Post #2 from [`LINKEDIN_POSTS_WEEK1.md`](../LINKEDIN_POSTS_WEEK1.md)
- Follow up on Day 8 replies with voice notes (4× higher conversion)

### Day 11-12 — Follow-up Week + First Demos

- Anyone who replied "DEMO" → schedule 15-min demo (use [`DEMO_FLOW.md`](DEMO_FLOW.md))
- Anyone who asked "How much?" → share pricing from [`PRICING_PLAYBOOK.md`](PRICING_PLAYBOOK.md)
- Send Follow-up F1 to Day 8 non-responders (4 days later)
- Continue fresh outreach (50 WhatsApp + 10 LinkedIn + 20 email daily)

### Day 13 — Publish Post #3 + Publish Blog

- LinkedIn Post #3 from [`LINKEDIN_POSTS_WEEK1.md`](../LINKEDIN_POSTS_WEEK1.md)
- Publish [`BLOG_POST_1.md`](../BLOG_POST_1.md) on celerapps.com/blog
- Share blog excerpt on LinkedIn (200 words max + link in first comment)

### Day 14 — Week 2 Review

Track these numbers:

| Metric | Target | Actual |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Messages sent | 560 | |
| Replies received | 45-67 (8-12%) | |
| "DEMO" responses | 6-10 | |
| Demos completed | 3-5 | |
| Hot leads (ready to buy) | 1-2 | |

**If reply rate < 5%**: Your templates need work. Personalize harder.
**If reply rate > 10%**: Double down on that channel/template.
**If 0 demos**: Your Loom video isn't compelling. Re-record.

## WEEK 3 — DEMO & CONVERT (Days 15-21)
## ════════════════════════════════════

### Day 15-17 — Demo Marathon

- Run all scheduled demos using [`DEMO_FLOW.md`](DEMO_FLOW.md)
- After each demo, send within 1 hour:
  1. WhatsApp thank-you with recap
  2. Offer to set up their tenant right now (5 min)
  3. If they hesitate → "Free 14 days, no card, cancel anytime"

### Day 18-19 — Convert Warm Leads

- Follow up on all demo attendees who didn't convert
- Use the objection handling from [`PRICING_PLAYBOOK.md`](PRICING_PLAYBOOK.md)
- Offer to set up their first tenant personally (white-glove onboarding)
- Continue fresh outreach (scale down to 30/day — focus on quality)

### Day 20-21 — Content + Iterate

- Publish a new LinkedIn post (pick from the "Future post buckets" in the guide)
- Write a case study if you have your first client
- Drop any template with < 5% reply rate
- Double spend on templates with > 10% reply rate
- Scrape 100 more prospects to refill the pipeline
